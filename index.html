Point of view

Life After AI

After the prompt

Outputs need reviewable systems.

Strong AI products make it easy to inspect, recover, refine, and learn from imperfect output.

After the prototype

Good demos are not enough.

What matters is whether ownership, workflow, and incentives can support the thing once it ships.

After the hype

Judgment becomes the differentiator.

The durable edge is clearer standards, better service quality, and more credible product decisions.
